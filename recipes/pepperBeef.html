Pepper Beef

Description:

Pepper beef is a classic Chinese recipe that is incredibly simple and quick to prepare, but packs a ton of flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 12oz Beef Flank Steak
  • 4 - 6 Green hot peppers (Anaheim will work)
  • 1 - 2 Red medium hot peppers
  • 1 teaspoon Dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Light soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 - 2 tablespoon Shaoxing cooking wine
  • 1/8 teaspoon White pepper
  • 3-4 cloves Garlic
